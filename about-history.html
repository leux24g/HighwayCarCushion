Company History
  1. About Us
  2. Company History

We are providing good services to our valuable customers

Highway Car Cushion Industries has over 30 years of experience in the automotive industry. We have been an Isuzu EA soft trim supplier for the past 25 years to date.

The company has diversified its expertise to iron and wood works. We can create beautiful and functional gates (which can be automated), doors, balcony, rails, frames and wall decoraters.

Highway Car Cushion continues to grow every day thanks to the confidence our clients have in us. We cover many industries such as Isuzu, Toyota as well as many others.

TIMELINE HISTORY

1990
The beginning of a legacy

This was the time when we started our company. We had no idea how far we would go, we were not even sure that we would be able to survive for a few years. What drove us to start the company was the understanding that we could provide a service no one else was providing.
